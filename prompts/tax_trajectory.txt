## TAX TRAJECTORY ANALYSIS RULES

### Conversion Window Determination
- The conversion window consists of years when the client's taxable income is near zero or low — typically between retirement and RMD age 73, especially before Social Security starts.
- Early retirement gap: years between retirement age and SS start age often have minimal income, creating a prime conversion window.
- Use the income projections to identify low-income years. Years with projected income of $0 or very low amounts are highest priority.
- The window closes at RMD start age 73 (federal law), when mandatory distributions inflate AGI.

### Social Security Benefit Taxation
- Provisional income = AGI + tax-exempt interest + 50% of SS annual benefits
- MFJ thresholds:
  - Below $32,000: 0% of SS is taxable
  - $32,001–$44,000: up to 50% of SS is taxable
  - Above $44,000: up to 85% of SS is taxable
- Roth conversions increase AGI and therefore provisional income, potentially making more SS taxable.

### IRMAA Risk Assessment
- IRMAA = Income-Related Monthly Adjustment Amount for Medicare Part B & D premiums
- MFJ IRMAA tiers (2026):
  - ≤$212,000: base premium ($185/month)
  - $212,001–$266,000: +$74/month surcharge
  - $266,001–$320,000: +$185/month surcharge
  - $320,001–$394,000: +$295.80/month surcharge
  - $394,001–$749,999: +$406.90/month surcharge
  - $750,000+: +$443.90/month surcharge
- IRMAA has a 2-year lookback: income in year Y affects Medicare premiums in year Y+2.
- To project IRMAA risk at RMD age:
  1. Apply 6% annual nominal growth to current pre-tax balance
  2. Compute years until age 73
  3. Calculate projected balance at 73
  4. Compute first RMD: projected_balance / 26.5 (IRS life expectancy factor at age 73)
  5. Add SS annual income + any other income
  6. Check if total exceeds $212,000 IRMAA Tier 1

### RMD Calculation
- RMD start age: 73 (SECURE 2.0)
- Formula: account_balance / life_expectancy_factor
- Life expectancy factor at age 73 = 26.5
- Default growth rate: 6% nominal per year
- Project pre-tax balance: current_pretax * (1.06)^years_until_rmd

### Urgency Classification
- urgency = "high" if ANY of:
  - Projected first RMD + other income crosses ANY IRMAA tier
  - Projected RMDs push client into higher tax bracket at retirement
  - Total pre-tax balance > $1,000,000
- urgency = "medium" if moderate conversion opportunity exists
- urgency = "low" if minimal risk or small pre-tax balances
